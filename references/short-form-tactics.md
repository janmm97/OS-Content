# How I Built: short-form-tactics.md

## What This Document Is

A platform-specific tactics guide covering TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. It goes deeper than the script-writer doc on how each platform's algorithm works, what content performs, and how to optimize for distribution — not just script quality.

The pipeline reads this in Stages 1 and 3a to choose the right ad concept and platform recommendations for each piece of content.

---

## Why It Matters

Platform behavior changes fast. A tactics doc that's 12 months old will send the pipeline toward strategies that no longer work. This should be updated at least twice a year, or after any major platform algorithm change.

---

## Step 1: Research with Perplexity

Run these queries. The platform-specific ones are the most important.

**Universal principles:**
```
What are the universal best practices for short-form video content that apply across all platforms in 2026? Cover: watch time, completion rate, hook timing, engagement signals, and how algorithms reward content.
```

**TikTok specifically:**
```
How does the TikTok algorithm work in 2026? What signals matter most for organic reach? What content formats, durations, and hook types perform best? Include any recent changes to the For You Page distribution system.
```

**Instagram Reels specifically:**
```
How does Instagram Reels distribution work in 2026? What's different from TikTok in terms of audience behavior, content formats, and algorithm signals? What performs best for Reels vs TikTok?
```

**YouTube Shorts specifically:**
```
How does YouTube Shorts distribution work in 2026? How does it differ from TikTok and Reels in terms of audience, content lifecycle, and what performs? What hook styles work on Shorts that don't work elsewhere?
```

**Trends for 2026:**
```
What are the biggest short-form video content trends in 2026? Include: format trends (hooks, transitions, overlays), content style trends, and algorithm-driven changes in what gets rewarded.
```

**AI-assisted content creation:**
```
How are successful creators and brands using AI tools to produce short-form video content at scale in 2026? Cover tools, workflows, and what AI does well vs where human judgment is still required.
```

**KPIs and measurement:**
```
What KPIs matter most for measuring short-form video performance in 2026? How do you distinguish a good hook from good content? What benchmarks exist for completion rate, saves, shares, and paid CTR?
```

## What a Good Version Includes

- Per-platform algorithm explanations (not just "post consistently")
- Specific hook timing data (e.g., "decision window is 1.7–3 seconds")
- Completion rate benchmarks per platform and format
- Clear differentiation between TikTok, Reels, and Shorts behavior
- Emerging format trends with examples
- KPI benchmarks you can use to evaluate your own content
